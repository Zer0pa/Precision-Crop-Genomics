## What We Do Not Claim

- Not a deployable medical product, not a regulatory certification, not a clinical or human-subject system. Outputs are research artifacts.
- Zer0pa does not take trait IP. Any sub-brief proposing Zer0pa take trait IP fails the brain-functionality gate at PRD review.
- Zer0pa does not perform environmental release of GMOs or gene-edited material directly. Physical wet-lab work is performed by partner organisations under their own biosafety regimes.
- South Africa is not a near-term ag deployment market for gene-edited products under the January 2024 Section-19 ministerial decision; SA strategy defaults to MAS-and-prediction pathways.
- v1 build does not cover herbicide tolerance traits, PIP-equivalent insect resistance via novel insecticidal protein, or pharma traits.
- Pangenome-aware modelling claims do not extend to species without public graph pangenomes; orphan crops fall back to SNP-only mode with explicit orphan_crop_pangenome_unavailable flag.
